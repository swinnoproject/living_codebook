    title

    The title of the source article.

    type

    A characterisation of the source content

    Artifactual_complexity

    art_comp

    What is the complexity of the innovation as artifact as opposed to the development of the innovation

    Commissioner

    commissioner

    Name of the agent company governmental agency etc that has commissioned the innovation

    Corporate_group

    corp

    In the SWINNO extension collection Name of the corporate group of the firm as stated in the source article

    Country

    country

    In which country was the collaborating actor located during the collaboration. ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes with amendments.

    desc_sv

    Description of the innovation in Swedish This may include the purpose or function of the innovation and what is new about it and how it works

    dev_comp

    firm_launched_development

    Was the firm founded to develop or commercialize the innovation

    Firm_only_produces_the_innovation

    prod_only

    If the firm recorded as Innovating firm produces the innovation but has not developed it this variable has the value 1

    Id

    id

    Unique, stable identifier for the link between innovation and article.

    innovation_id

    year

    The year in which the article was published.
